## Key Insights

- Mutual fund NAVs showed consistent long-term growth.
- SBI Mutual Fund maintained the highest AUM during the study period.
- SIP inflows increased significantly between 2022 and 2025.
- Flexi Cap and Large & Mid Cap categories attracted higher investments.
- Most investors preferred SIP over lump-sum investments.
- Metro cities contributed a significant share of transactions.
- Portfolio allocations were concentrated in Banking, IT, Pharma, and Utilities sectors.
- Benchmark indices displayed varying performance across market segments.
